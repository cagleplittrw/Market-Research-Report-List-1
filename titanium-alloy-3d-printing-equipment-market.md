<p><strong>The Titanium Alloy 3D Printing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SLM</li><li>LSF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The titanium alloy 3D printing equipment market includes two primary technologies: Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Laser Sintering Filament (LSF). SLM utilizes high-powered lasers to melt and fuse titanium powder layer by layer, resulting in highly dense and complex parts suitable for aerospace and medical applications. In contrast, LSF involves extruding titanium-infused filament through a heated nozzle, allowing for lower-cost production and broader design flexibility but typically with lower precision and mechanical properties than SLM.</p></p>
