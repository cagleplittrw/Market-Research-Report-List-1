<p><p>The Household Ventilator Mask market features two primary types: the oronasal mask and the full face mask. The oronasal mask covers only the nose and mouth, allowing for easier communication and less claustrophobia, making it suitable for patients who breathe primarily through their mouth. In contrast, the full face mask covers the entire face, ensuring secure airflow for individuals with nasal congestion or those requiring higher respiratory support. Both types cater to different patient comfort and therapeutic needs in home ventilation systems.</p></p>
