<p><p>The Point-of-Entry (POE) water purifiers market encompasses various filtration technologies, including Reverse Osmosis (RO) and Ultrafiltration (UF) membranes, along with other purification methods. RO systems use a semi-permeable membrane to eliminate contaminants, producing highly purified water. Ultrafiltration employs a membrane that allows water and small molecules to pass while blocking larger impurities. Other technologies may include activated carbon filters or water softeners, each catering to different purification needs based on water quality and consumer preferences.</p></p>
