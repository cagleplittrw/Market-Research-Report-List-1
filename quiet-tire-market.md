<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quiet tire market can be categorized into radial and bias tire types. Radial tires feature belted layers that run perpendicular to the tire's direction of travel, offering better traction, improved fuel efficiency, and reduced noise on the road. In contrast, bias tires have layers that crisscross, providing a sturdier structure but often resulting in higher noise levels and less fuel efficiency. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with radial tires generally favored for modern vehicles seeking comfort and performance.</p></p>
