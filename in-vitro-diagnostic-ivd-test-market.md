<p><p>The In Vitro Diagnostic (IVD) test market encompasses various types, each serving distinct diagnostic purposes. Immuno diagnostics utilize antibodies for detecting biomarkers, often in diseases like infections or autoimmune disorders. Chemistry diagnostics analyze chemical components in blood and other fluids for metabolic disorders. Molecular diagnostics focus on genetic material to identify diseases at a cellular level. Point-of-Care Testing (POCT) provides immediate results at the testing site, enhancing patient management. Other markets include microbiological and histopathological tests, expanding diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
