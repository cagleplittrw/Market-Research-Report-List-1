<p><ul><li>Three Layers Engineered Wooded Flooring</li><li>Multilayer Engineered Wooded Flooring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engineered wood market comprises primarily two types: three-layer engineered wood flooring and multilayer engineered wood flooring. Three-layer engineered wood flooring consists of a top hardwood veneer, a middle layer of plywood, and a bottom support layer, offering stability and aesthetics. Multilayer engineered wood flooring, on the other hand, features multiple layers of wood, enhancing durability and moisture resistance. Both types provide a sustainable alternative to solid wood, catering to varying preferences in design, functionality, and budget.</p></p>
