<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Warning Sound System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Warning Sound System</li><li>Passive Warning Sound System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Warning Sound System market is divided into two main types: Active Warning Sound Systems and Passive Warning Sound Systems. Active Warning Sound Systems generate sounds actively when the vehicle is moving at low speeds, alerting pedestrians of its presence. In contrast, Passive Warning Sound Systems depend on existing ambient sounds, such as tire noise, to inform bystanders. Both systems enhance safety for pedestrians, particularly those with visual impairments, while promoting the adoption of electric vehicles.</p></p>
