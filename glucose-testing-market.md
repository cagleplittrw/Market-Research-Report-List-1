<p><p>The glucose testing market is primarily divided into minimal invasive and non-invasive testing segments. Minimal invasive testing involves techniques that require small tissue penetration, such as fingerstick blood tests, providing accurate glucose levels with minimal discomfort. On the other hand, non-invasive testing eliminates the need for skin penetration, utilizing technologies like optical sensors or electromagnetic fields to measure glucose levels. These advancements aim to enhance patient convenience and comfort while maintaining reliable glucose monitoring for diabetes management.</p></p>
