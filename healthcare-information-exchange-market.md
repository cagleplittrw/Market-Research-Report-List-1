<p><p>Healthcare Information Exchange (HIE) market types include Centralized, Decentralized, and Hybrid Models. Centralized Models consolidate data into a single repository, allowing easy access but raising concerns about data security. Decentralized Models enable independent systems to share information without central storage, promoting data control but complicating interoperability. Hybrid Models combine elements of both, offering flexibility in data management while addressing security and accessibility challenges. These models aim to enhance the sharing of patient information across diverse healthcare settings, improving care coordination.</p></p>
