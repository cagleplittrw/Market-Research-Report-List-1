<p><strong>The Fruits Drying Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Drying Machine</li><li>Vacuum Drying Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fruits drying machine market is primarily categorized into atmospheric drying machines and vacuum drying machines. Atmospheric drying machines utilize heat and air circulation to remove moisture from fruits at normal atmospheric pressure, making them energy-efficient and widely used. In contrast, vacuum drying machines operate under reduced pressure, allowing for lower temperature drying, which helps preserve flavors, colors, and nutrients. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the food processing industry, impacting quality and shelf life.</p></p>
