<p><p>The panoramic security camera market encompasses two primary types: IP cameras and analog cameras. IP cameras leverage digital technology to transmit video over the internet, enabling remote access, high-definition footage, and advanced features like motion detection and analytics. In contrast, analog cameras rely on traditional coaxial cables for video transmission, offering a more cost-effective solution, but with lower image quality and limited functionality. Both types serve diverse applications, from residential security to large-scale surveillance systems, catering to varying user needs and budgets.</p></p>
