<p><p>The grease fittings market is categorized into three main types based on the angle of connection: straight, 45-degree, and 90-degree fittings. Straight fittings allow for direct lubrication access, making them ideal for tight spaces. The 45-degree fittings provide a slight angle for easier alignment in constrained areas, while 90-degree fittings enable lubrication at right angles, enhancing accessibility in complex machinery. Each type serves specific applications, ensuring efficient maintenance by facilitating effective grease distribution in various mechanical systems.</p></p>
