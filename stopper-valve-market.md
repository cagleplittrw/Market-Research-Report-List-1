<p><p>The stopper valve market primarily includes globe valves and needle valves, each serving distinct functions in fluid control. Globe valves are designed for regulating flow, offering a straightforward mechanism that allows precise adjustments suitable for varying pressure conditions. In contrast, needle valves feature a thin, tapered point, enabling fine control over small flow rates, ideal for applications requiring delicacy. Both types are essential in industries like oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing, ensuring efficient and reliable flow management.</p></p>
