<p><p>The medical device adhesive market consists of various types, each serving specific applications. Cyanoacrylates provide quick bonding and are suitable for skin and tissue adhesion. Acrylic adhesives offer strong bonds and are often used for device assembly. Epoxy adhesives deliver excellent durability and resistance, ideal for load-bearing applications. Silicone adhesives provide flexibility and biocompatibility, making them suitable for sensitive applications. Polyurethane adhesives combine strength and flexibility, often used in devices requiring durability and resistance to environmental factors.</p></p>
