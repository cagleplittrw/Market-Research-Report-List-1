<p><strong>The Cold Bend Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-Point Bend</li><li>4-Point Bend</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cold Bend Testing Machine market mainly consists of two methods: 3-Point Bend and 4-Point Bend testing. The 3-Point Bend test involves applying a load at a single center point on a specimen supported at both ends, providing insights into material strength and flexibility. In contrast, the 4-Point Bend test applies loads at two points, distributing the stress more evenly and allowing for a more thorough evaluation of material properties. Both types are essential for assessing materials in industries like construction, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
