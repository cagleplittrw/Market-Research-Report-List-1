<p><p>Engineered Quartz (E-Quartz) is a synthetic material composed of 90-95% crushed natural quartz crystals mixed with resin and pigments. The market can be categorized into Phaneromer Quartz and Cryptocrystalline Quartz. Phaneromer Quartz features larger crystal structures, offering a more natural stone appearance and aesthetic appeal, while Cryptocrystalline Quartz consists of tiny, interlocking crystals, enhancing durability and providing varied color options. Both types cater to diverse applications in countertops, flooring, and wall panels, appealing to design and functionality preferences.</p></p>
