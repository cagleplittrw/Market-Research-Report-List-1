<p><p>The radar front end market is categorized into various frequency bands, with S-Band and X-Band being prominent segments. X-Band, covering 8 to 12 GHz, offers higher resolution and is favored in applications such as military radar, air surveillance, and tracking systems, where fine detail is crucial.</p></p>
