<p><strong>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Shredder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-voltage Electrostatic Separation Type</li><li>Non-high voltage Electrostatic Separation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB shredder market is categorized into two main types based on separation technology: high-voltage electrostatic separation and non-high voltage electrostatic separation. High-voltage electrostatic separation types utilize elevated voltages to effectively separate metals from non-metals, enhancing recovery rates of valuable materials like copper and gold. In contrast, non-high voltage electrostatic separation types employ standard voltages for material separation, often resulting in lower efficiency. Each type serves specific recycling needs, balancing cost, effectiveness, and safety in processing printed circuit boards.</p></p>
