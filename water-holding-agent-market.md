<p><p>The Water Holding Agent market is primarily segmented into two types: liquid and solid. Solid water holding agents, often in granular or powder form, absorb moisture and release it slowly to the surrounding soil. Both types are essential for improving water availability in cultivation, reducing irrigation frequency, and supporting plant growth, especially in arid environments.</p></p>
