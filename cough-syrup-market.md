<p><p>The cough syrup market comprises three primary types: cough suppressants, expectorants, and antihistamines. Cough suppressants work by reducing the urge to cough, offering relief from dry coughs. Expectorants help thin and loosen mucus in the airways, facilitating easier expulsion and providing relief from productive coughs. Antihistamines address coughs caused by allergies by blocking histamine receptors, thereby reducing related symptoms. Together, these categories cater to diverse consumer needs, making the cough syrup market a vital segment of the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
