<p><p>A nerve conduit is a medical device used to facilitate the regeneration of nerve tissues, primarily in cases of nerve injuries or diseases. These conduits support and guide the growth of axons across a gap or damaged area, promoting healing and restoration of nerve function. Commonly made from biocompatible materials, nerve conduits serve as a scaffold and can be filled with growth factors or cellular components to enhance regeneration.</p><p>The nerve conduit market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of neurological disorders and the rising number of surgical procedures aimed at repairing nerve damage. Advances in biomaterials and tissue engineering are further propelling market expansion, as these innovations improve the effectiveness and functionality of nerve conduits. The demand for minimally invasive surgeries has also contributed to growth in this sector, as nerve conduits are often designed for such procedures.</p><p>The Nerve Conduit Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.8% during the forecast period.
