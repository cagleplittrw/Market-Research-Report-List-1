<p><p>The riveting tools market comprises three primary types: pneumatic riveting tools, battery-operated riveting tools, and hand-operated riveting tools. Pneumatic riveting tools utilize compressed air for high-speed fastening, making them ideal for industrial applications. Battery-operated riveting tools offer portability and convenience, allowing for use in remote locations without a power supply. Hand-operated riveting tools require manual effort, providing a more economical option for small-scale projects or DIY tasks. Each type caters to different user needs and operational contexts.</p></p>
