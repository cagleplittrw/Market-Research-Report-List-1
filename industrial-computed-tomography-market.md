<p><p>The Industrial Computed Tomography (CT) market is categorized into three main types: High Energy Industrial CT, Low Energy Industrial CT, and Mini-Focus Industrial CT. High Energy Industrial CT is used for dense materials, offering detailed insights into complex structures. Low Energy Industrial CT focuses on softer materials, providing clearer imaging for components like plastics and ceramics.
