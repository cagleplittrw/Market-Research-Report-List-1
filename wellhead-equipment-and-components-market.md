<p><p>The wellhead equipment and components market encompasses essential fittings used in oil and gas production. Hangers support and align the casing in wells, ensuring structural integrity. Flanges facilitate the connection between pipes and equipment, providing leak-proof seals. The master valve serves as a critical control component, regulating the flow of fluids and isolating the wellbore.
