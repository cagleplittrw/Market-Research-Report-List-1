<p><p>The audio baby monitors market is primarily divided into two types: wired and wireless audio baby monitors. Wired audio baby monitors connect directly to a power source and the parent unit via cables, providing a reliable and interference-free audio connection. In contrast, wireless audio baby monitors utilize radio frequencies or Wi-Fi technology to transmit sound, offering greater mobility and convenience for parents. Both types cater to different preferences, with wired monitors focusing on stability and wireless monitors emphasizing flexibility.</p></p>
