<p><p>The skeletal deformation correction market encompasses various surgical procedures aimed at correcting deformities in the bones. Osteotomy involves cutting and repositioning bones to realign them. Limb reconstruction focuses on restoring function and appearance through surgical interventions. Arthrodesis involves fusing joints to stabilize and relieve pain, while arthroplasty replaces damaged joints with artificial components. Vertebral column resection is a complex procedure to remove parts of the spine to correct severe deformities, often improving mobility and quality of life.</p></p>
