<p><p>The non-phthalate catalysts market is segmented into supported and unsupported catalysts. Supported catalysts are those that are attached to a carrier material, enhancing stability and activity, commonly used in processes like polymerization and oxidation. Unsupported catalysts, on the other hand, function independently without a carrier, offering high surface area and reactivity for specific reactions. Both types play crucial roles in producing eco-friendly alternatives to phthalate-based chemicals in various industries, including plastics, coatings, and adhesives.</p></p>
