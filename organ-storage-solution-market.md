<p><strong>The Organ Storage Solution Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Storage Solution</li><li>Perfusion Storage Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organ storage solution market is primarily categorized into two types: static storage solutions and perfusion storage solutions. Static storage involves cool preservation techniques that maintain organ viability through chilled conditions without active blood flow, typically used for short-term storage. In contrast, perfusion storage solutions utilize a continuous blood or nutrient supply, enhancing oxygenation and nutrient delivery during preservation, which supports longer storage times and improved organ function upon transplantation. Both methods are vital for optimizing organ viability before transplantation.</p></p>
