<p><p>Remote Patient Monitoring Software is categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, enabling healthcare providers to access patient data from anywhere, promoting scalability and ease of collaboration. In contrast, on-premises software is installed directly on a healthcare facility's local servers, providing enhanced control over data security and compliance but often requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Both types serve to improve patient engagement and streamline chronic disease management.</p></p>
