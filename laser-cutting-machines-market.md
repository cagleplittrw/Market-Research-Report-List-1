<p><p>The laser cutting machines market includes three primary types: CO2 laser cutting machines, fiber laser cutting machines, and others. CO2 laser machines utilize carbon dioxide as the gas medium, making them ideal for cutting non-metal materials like wood and plastics. Fiber laser cutting machines, known for their efficiency and precision, use fiber optics and are particularly effective for metal cutting, including stainless steel and aluminum. The "others" category encompasses various specialized machines and technologies, catering to niche applications.</p></p>
